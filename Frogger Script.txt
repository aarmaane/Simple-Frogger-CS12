Welcome to Frogger! Your objective is to get into all 5 openings at the end of the river without
coming into contact with any cars or snakes! Be sure to avoid drowning in the river by hopping on logs and turtles.
Once you reach all 5 openings you promote to the next level. Hurry! You only have 30 seconds for
each opening. Once you lose all 3 of your lives it's Game Over! Good Luck!
